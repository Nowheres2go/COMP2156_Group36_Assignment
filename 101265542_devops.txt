Course Description:
This course provides a comprehensive introduction to the principles and practices of DevOps.
It covers the collaboration between software development and IT operations, aiming to improve the lifecycle of systems development and ensuring agile, continuous delivery of high-quality software.
Students will learn about automation, process re-engineering, and technology tools that facilitate DevOps practices.

Objectives:
Understand the fundamentals of DevOps and its significance in modern software development and IT operations.
Gain hands-on experience with tools and technologies commonly used in DevOps ecosystems.
Learn about continuous integration (CI), continuous delivery (CD), and continuous deployment strategies.
Explore monitoring, logging, and communication tools that support DevOps practices.
Develop skills to automate builds, tests, deployments, and infrastructure management.
